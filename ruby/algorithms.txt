Washing Dishes

I.	Grab Sponge
II. For every dirty dish in your sink:
		1. if sponge needs soap:
			put soap on sponge

		2.  While dish is not clean:
			i. scrub with sponge
			ii. if sponge is too soft for certain gunk:
					Use the scrub pad instead

		3.	Place clean dish on dryer rack 

III. For every clean dish in the dryer rack:
		1.  While dish is not fully dry: 
				i.	Gyrate towel over face of dish
		2.  Put dish away in its cabinet
